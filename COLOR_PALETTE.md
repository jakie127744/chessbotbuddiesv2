## Rationale
- **Contrast & readability:** Charcoal bases with light-steel text keep WCAG-friendly contrast while reducing glare.
- **Brand warmth:** Amber primary adds friendly “buddy” energy and makes CTAs obvious without neon blue fatigue.
- **Calm secondary:** Slate blue handles links/secondary actions, pairing well with amber and chess blues.
- **Board focus:** Neutral blues for squares plus a soft shadow keep the board the hero without heavy drop shadows.
- **Hierarchy:** Slightly lighter surfaces and stripes for sidebars/history panes give depth and scan-ability.
- **Light mode match:** A soft, low-saturation light scheme reuses amber for CTA consistency.
