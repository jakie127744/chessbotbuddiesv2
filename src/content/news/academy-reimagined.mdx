<p className="mb-4">
    We are thrilled to unveil a significant update to the Chess Academy at ChessBotBuddies. This release represents a complete structural reimagining of our educational platform, designed to provide a more immersive, structured, and effective learning experience for students of all ages.
</p>

<h3 className="text-xl font-bold text-white mt-8 mb-4">From Categories to Worlds</h3>
<p className="mb-4">
    Our previous category-based system has been transformed into a thematic "World" progression. This changelog details the key differences between the legacy platform and our new release:
</p>
<ul className="list-disc pl-6 space-y-2 mb-6 marker:text-sky-400">
    <li>
        <strong className="text-white">Legacy Version:</strong> Grouped lessons into broad "Beginner" and "Intermediate" lists.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong className="text-white">New Version:</strong> Introduces <strong>World 1 (Chess Basics)</strong> and <strong>World 2 (The Tactical Forest)</strong>. This structured path ensures students master prerequisites before advancing to complex concepts.
    </li>
</ul>

<h3 className="text-xl font-bold text-white mt-8 mb-4">Enhanced Tactical Training</h3>
<p className="mb-4">In World 2, we have overhauled how tactics are taught:</p>
<ul className="list-disc pl-6 space-y-2 mb-6 marker:text-sky-400">
    <li>
        <strong className="text-white">Step-by-Step Instruction:</strong> We moved away from simple puzzle solving to a "Guided Walkthrough" format. For complex concepts like <em>Overloading</em> or <em>Deflection</em>, students are now guided move-by-move through "textbook examples."
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong className="text-white">Comprehensive Minigame Suite:</strong> Theory requires practice. We have deployed <strong>10 specialized minigames</strong>, one for each tactical theme. From <em>Fork Frenzy</em> to <em>Overload Ops</em>, these targeted challenges allow students to drill specific patterns.
    </li>
</ul>

<h3 className="text-xl font-bold text-white mt-8 mb-4">Commitment to Quality and Safety</h3>
<ul className="list-disc pl-6 space-y-2 mb-6 marker:text-sky-400">
    <li>
        <strong className="text-white">Audited Content:</strong> We conducted a comprehensive audit of our entire puzzle database. Every lesson challenge and minigame puzzle has been manually verified for tactical soundness.
    </li>
    <li>
        <strong className="text-white">Safety First:</strong> We maintain a strictly safe environment for children with <strong>no open chat</strong> and <strong>no user tracking</strong>. Students can jump straight into the action—registration is only requested after playing to save their progress, ensuring a barrier-free start to learning.
    </li>
</ul>

<p className="mt-8 italic text-zinc-400 border-l-4 border-zinc-700 pl-4">
    This update bridges the gap between learning the rules and mastering the game. We invite educators, parents, and students to explore the new World 2 and experience the next generation of chess education.
</p>
